WHAT WE DO?



What We Do?

We are a group of volunteers at Hillcrest High School who are interested in the area of Composting and want to help the soil of many lands through this process. We truly believe that taking care of our environment is an essential part of our lives and we want to make that dream a reality. By using vegetable scraps that nobody uses in cooking, we are not only saving the world from litter but we are also helping the soil so plants next year have a good foundation.




Things Needed for Composting

Composting is a job that requires pretty specific tools. One incorrect ingredient could cause harm to the soil. Here are the ingredients that we will use:
1. Vegetable waste
2. Eggshells
3. Coffee grounds
4. Hay and Straw
5. Tree twigs
