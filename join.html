Why Join Us?

We are a young group of volunteers who wish to get involved in composting. We are interested in expanding our club. We wish to get other people whether experienced or not to help us in this task. Commitment is completely optional and volunteers can quit any time if they realize that composting isn't for them. You don't need to be experienced to join. There will be plenty of people to teach so all you need to do is come. Equipment will be provided. If you have any equipment such as shovels or other items, please bring them as they will help out. Please fill out the form below if interested in joining.
