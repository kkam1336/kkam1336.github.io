About Us

navigate_next
Starting as a small group from Hillcrest High School, we wish to expand our love for the environment and planet by taking care of the planet. We do this through Composting. We want to get more people involved in Composting and learning so that we can be more efficient when it comes to the task. We want to teach our community the importance of our environment and by doing small things such as composting, we can help the environment in the long run.

How do we stay so cheap?

Red Rock Airlines stays so cheap in a variety of ways. We operate only Boeing 737s so our staff only needs to be trained for one aircraft. The Boeing 737 is also extremely fuel efficient, burning 0.01 gallons of fuel per person per mile. By using the 737, Red Rock Airlines saves money on training and fuel, allowing us to bring you the convenience you deserve.


How are we so convenient?

As an airline company, we understand that we need to get our customers to the places they want to go. So, we prioritize efficiency and make sure to get you to where you want to go in the fastest, most convenient way possible. We purposely reduce our time between flights to about 30-45 minutes so we spend more time in the air, taking our customers where they want to go; wasting as little time as possible. By being time efficient we can deliver cheaper flights that still have the conveniences you expect.


What services do we provide?

We provide convenient air transportation in the western region of the United States. Our services are available in Washington, Oregon, California, Nevada, Arizona, Utah, Idaho, Montana, Wyoming, Colorado. Our flights are available to and from any of these states. Our aircrafts cater to handicapped passengers as well as those who are blind and deaf. Complimentary snacks and drinks are provided on all flights.
